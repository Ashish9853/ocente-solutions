Privacy policy

Data Collection & Privacy Policy

At Ocente Solutions, we respect your privacy and are committed to handling your personal data responsibly. Below is an overview of how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with Indian data protection regulations: Purpose of Data Collection We collect your data to:
• Provide Customized Services: To offer IT solutions and support tailored to your needs.
• Improve User Experience: To optimize our website and services based on your feedback.
• Marketing & Communication: To send you relevant updates and offers (you can opt-out anytime).
• Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and ensure data security.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with services like hosting, data analysis, and marketing. These third parties are required to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal requirements, or resolve disputes. Once the data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

User Rights

You have the right to:

•Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
• Rectify: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Delete: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
• Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time by opting out.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers. We regularly review our security practices to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your information.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011. We are committed to adhering to industry standards for data protection and user privacy. By providing your personal information, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your data as outlined in this policy. If you have any concerns or would like to exercise your rights, please contact us at info@ocentesolution.com
